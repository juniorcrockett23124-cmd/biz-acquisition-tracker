## Why It Fits

This is one of the strongest current MSP targets because the listing combines recurring cybersecurity-led revenue, a meaningful active-client base, 3 full-time employees, and explicit retirement motivation.

## Revenue And Cash Flow View

- Recurring revenue signal: Strong
- Team signal: Moderate

## Key Risks

The main concern is valuation discipline. At a $2.1M asking price, the business needs proven customer retention, limited owner dependence, and a clean team handoff to justify the premium.

## Financing View
