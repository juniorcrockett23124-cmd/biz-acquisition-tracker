# Source Discovery - 2026-06-22

## Goal

Stand up a repeatable process for expanding beyond the current core sources without polluting daily sourcing with unproven channels.

## Current Active Sources

- BizBuySell
- BusinessesForSale
- SMB.co

## Immediate Trial Queue

- BizQuest
  - Reason to trial: broad marketplace coverage and already named in the intended source stack
  - What to validate: whether listings consistently disclose enough revenue, cash flow, staffing, and seller-motivation detail

- BusinessBroker.net
  - Reason to trial: another broad marketplace that may surface smaller service businesses missed elsewhere
  - What to validate: listing freshness, detail depth, and noise from low-quality broker reposts

- niche accounting practice brokers
  - Reason to trial: likely strongest category fit for recurring revenue, retiree-owner transitions, and team-based service firms
  - What to validate: whether deal flow skews too large, too small, or off-market without enough public detail

- niche MSP / IT services brokers
  - Reason to trial: likely better fit than general marketplaces for durable managed-services businesses
  - What to validate: owner dependence, customer concentration, and whether listings actually disclose contract quality

## Lower-Priority Trials

- Axial
  - Useful for somewhat larger lower-middle-market deals, but may sit above the current acquisition sweet spot

- Acquire.com
  - Useful only when software-like recurring service businesses fit the buy box; not a broad default source

- LoopNet
  - Useful only when business value is bundled with real estate; not a core operating-business source

## Process Change

- New-source discovery now lives in `jobs/discover-sources.md`
- Source status is tracked in `data/source-registry.md`
- Daily sourcing should prioritize `active` sources first and selectively test `trial` sources
