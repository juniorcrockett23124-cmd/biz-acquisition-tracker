Small CPA and bookkeeping practices are usually stable if client relationships transfer and the buyer keeps service quality high through tax season. Local tax/bookkeeping practices can be resilient, but growth is often modest.

## What Is Dangerous

- Seasonal concentration around tax deadlines.
- Seller relationship risk if clients are loyal to one preparer.
- Limited public detail on client concentration and staffing depth.

## Must-Ask Diligence Questions

- What percentage of revenue is annual tax prep only?
