# Research Memo - Fairfax County Full Service CPA & Bookkeeping

## Core Thesis

This looks like a potentially strong platform-quality accounting listing, but the richer asking price means transition risk and client retention have to be underwritten more carefully.

## Market And Industry Notes

Northern Virginia accounting practices can benefit from dense SMB demand and higher-income clients, but labor costs are also higher and talent retention matters more. Higher-value advisory work can improve quality if it is not owner locked.

## What Is Attractive

- Meaningful revenue and cash flow.
- Broad service mix can deepen client stickiness.
- Long operating history.

## What Is Dangerous

- Price is relatively full.
- Client loyalty may sit heavily with the senior CPA/owner.
- Talent retention in higher-cost markets can be harder.

## Must-Ask Diligence Questions

- How many licensed CPAs and bookkeepers are on staff?
- What percentage of revenue is recurring monthly work?
- What is the average tenure of top employees?
- Is there seller carry or transition support?
