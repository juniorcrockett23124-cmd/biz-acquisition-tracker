# Research Memo - High Margin 24-Year Managed IT Services Provider

## Core Thesis

This is one of the more interesting current listing-backed MSPs because the operating history and cash flow are strong, but it still needs proof that the margins are not simply owner compression in disguise.

## Market And Industry Notes

Long-lived MSPs with stable contracts can be excellent acquisition targets. The key is whether the owner is the primary sales and technical brain, or whether the business has real account management and delivery infrastructure.

## What Is Attractive

- Strong cash flow.
- 24-year operating history.
- Recurring managed IT model.

## What Is Dangerous

- High asking price.
- Team structure is unclear.
- Margin quality may depend on underinvestment or owner overwork.

## Must-Ask Diligence Questions

- How many contracted clients and what is average contract length?
- What is the role of the owner in sales and escalations?
- Are technicians W2 or contractor?
- What does the customer concentration curve look like?
