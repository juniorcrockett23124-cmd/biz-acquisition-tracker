# Outreach / Intro Questions

## Initial Broker or Seller Questions

1. Is the owner retiring, and if so what is the timeline?
2. How old is the owner?
3. How many employees are in place today, and what are their roles?
4. What percentage of revenue is recurring or contract-based?
5. What was last-twelve-month revenue and seller discretionary earnings / cash flow?
6. How owner-dependent is the business day to day?
7. Is seller financing available?
8. Are there any major customer concentration risks?
